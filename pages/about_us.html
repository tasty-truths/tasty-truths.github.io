Tasty Truths is all about helping you find that perfect recipe hassle free. Our team consists of a group of hard working students from Drexel, who were tired of trying to find recipes that were blocked behind paywalls or hundreds of diffrent ads and wanted to make a change.


We disgned Tasty Truths for a easy and interactive experiance for our users. From easy to navigate menues to the ability rate and leave a review, Tasty Truths has it all. On Tatsy Truths, Users have the ability to express their creative side with recipes they have created or changed. People can than interact with these recipes to give feedback and suggestions or just give complements to the chef for their amazing creations.


If users are looking for more of a social involvment, Tasty Truths can help also. Our blog pages allows users to share their experiances in the kitchen for other users to see. You can ask for reccomendations or for help if you are stuck and don't know why something turned out the way it did. Tasty Truths also has a contact page which can help you reach out to diffrent chefs about questions or partnering up on a recipe.
